How It Works

Nominations & Voting Process

  • Only selected films will be eligible for awards
  • Finalists will be nominated by the TAFFA jury panel
  • Public voting contributes to People's Choice Awards
  • Winners announced at the grand ceremony

Award Categories

Best Feature Film

Recognizing outstanding storytelling and production quality in full-length films

Best Short Film

Celebrating creativity and impact in short-form storytelling

Best Director

Honoring visionary leadership and artistic direction

Best Actor/Actress

Recognizing exceptional performances that bring characters to life

Best Cinematography

Celebrating visual artistry and technical excellence

Best Screenplay

Honoring compelling narratives and dialogue mastery

Best Emerging Talent

Supporting and recognizing promising new voices in cinema

Jury's Choice

Special recognition for outstanding artistic achievement

Audience Award

Chosen by festival attendees for films that resonate with viewers
