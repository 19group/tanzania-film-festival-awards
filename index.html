About the Festival

TAFFA is a powerful platform championing the film industry as a catalyst for social transformation and economic growth. Held annually, TAFFA unites filmmakers, media professionals, policymakers, investors, development partners, and the public for a three-month-long celebration of cinema, culture, and collaboration.
